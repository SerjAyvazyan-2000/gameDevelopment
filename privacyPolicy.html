Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how GameDev English collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to keeping your data safe and secure.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Demographic information (location, language preferences)
  • Account login information (username, password)
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions on site, IP address)
  • Any messages or inquiries you send us

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important updates and promotional content
  • Analyze and improve the functionality of our website
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

How We Protect Your Data

We implement industry-standard security measures such as SSL encryption, secure servers, and access controls to ensure that your personal information is not lost, misused, or accessed without authorization.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like analytics, payment processors, or learning platforms. These third parties are only permitted to use your data for specific purposes and are required to protect it appropriately.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide you with services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Request a copy of the data we hold

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted.
