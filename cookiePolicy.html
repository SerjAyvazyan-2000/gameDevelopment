Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how GameDev English uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently.

Why Do We Use Cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To ensure the website functions properly
  • To enhance user experience by remembering preferences
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To provide personalized content and ads

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the website to function.
  • Performance cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember user preferences and choices.
  • Targeting cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising.

How You Can Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

Third-Party Cookies

In some cases, we may use third-party services that set cookies on our behalf. For example, we use analytics services like Google Analytics to better understand user behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to the cookies we use or for other legal or regulatory reasons.
